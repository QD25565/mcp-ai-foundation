üéØ Why MCP-AI Foundation?

Built for Production

Every tool is optimized for minimal token usage and maximum performance. DuckDB backend, native arrays, and efficient caching ensure sub-second responses even with thousands of notes.

AI-First Design

No human-friendly decorations. Pipe-delimited output, compact timestamps, and only essential data. Tools that understand how AIs actually work.

Seamless Integration

Cross-tool auto-logging means your tasks automatically become notes, your notes reference entities, and everything connects through a knowledge graph.
